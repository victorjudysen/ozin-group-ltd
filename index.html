About Ozin Group Limited

Professional building contractors committed to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction

Professional Excellence Since 2024

Ozin Group Limited stands as Tanzania's premier Class 3 building contractor, strategically headquartered in Dodoma. Under the visionary leadership of Managing Director Omary Chuguma, we have rapidly established ourselves as the industry benchmark for construction excellence and professional integrity.

Our comprehensive approach encompasses residential construction, commercial development, infrastructure renovation, and specialized site preparation services. We leverage cutting-edge construction methodologies, stringent quality assurance protocols, and sustainable building practices to deliver projects that exceed international standards.

With our deep understanding of Tanzania's regulatory environment and local market dynamics, we provide strategic construction solutions that combine global best practices with regional expertise, ensuring optimal project outcomes for our distinguished clientele.

Our Mission

To transform Tanzania's construction landscape through innovative building solutions, uncompromising quality standards, and sustainable development practices that create lasting value for our clients and communities.

Our Services

Residential Construction
